What's "Marking up" in the Media Tenor World?


Marking up means the process how our employees search/find/extract relevant branch/combined or Detail based articles in certain medias like newspapers/TV/internet. Relevant are articles which contains MT-Clients, topics/themes or protagonists or companies from branches (like insurances or banks) with more than 5 lines / 5 seconds. In some media we also capture cited medias.
There are different methods in media-analysis to catch/collect such articles and to provide them:
- Clippings
- Search-Strings, Search-Engines and Fundstellenchecks
- Marking-up (individualized Media-Searching)
All methods have pro and cons. The marking-up by skilled Media-Analysts is by far the accurate way with the highest hit and lowest error ratio.
